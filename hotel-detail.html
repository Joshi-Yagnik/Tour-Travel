Rocky Mountains Adventure

7 Days / 6 Nights Vancouver, Canada Max 12 People 4.9 (128 reviews)

Tour Overview

Experience the breathtaking grandeur of Canada's Rocky Mountains in this 7-day adventure designed for those who seek both thrill and tranquility. From the shimmering turquoise waters of Lake Louise to the towering peaks of Banff National Park, this journey offers some of North America's most iconic landscapes.

Led by expert local guides, you'll hike glacier-carved valleys, kayak pristine mountain lakes, witness wildlife in its natural habitat and marvel at the dancing Northern Lights. Whether you're an avid hiker or a first-time explorer, this tour delivers an unforgettable experience.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Vancouver — Welcome Dinner

Arrive at Vancouver International Airport where your guide will greet you. Transfer to your mountain resort hotel. In the evening, join a welcome orientation dinner and meet your fellow travelers. Overnight at Mountain View Resort.

Arrival Resort Check-in Welcome Dinner
Day 2
Banff National Park — Hike & Gondola

Full day exploring Banff National Park. Morning hike through Johnston Canyon, afternoon Sulphur Mountain Gondola ride for 360° mountain views. Spot grizzly bears and elk in the wild.

Hiking Gondola Wildlife
Day 3
Lake Louise — Kayaking & Glacier Walk

Visit the iconic turquoise Lake Louise. Morning kayaking session on the lake, afternoon Glacier Walk on the Victoria Glacier with professional guides. Stunning photography opportunities throughout.

Kayaking Glacier Walk
Day 4
Icefields Parkway — Columbia Icefield

Scenic drive along the legendary Icefields Parkway — consistently ranked one of the world's most beautiful drives. Visit the Athabasca Glacier and the Glacier Skywalk glass platform.

Scenic Drive Photography
Day 5
Jasper National Park — Wildlife Safari

Morning wildlife safari in Jasper National Park. High chance of spotting moose, caribou, and mountain goats. Afternoon at Pyramid Lake — canoeing and relaxation.

Wildlife Safari Canoeing
Day 6
Northern Lights Experience & Bonfire Night

Daytime leisure and visit to local Banff town. In the evening, venture into the wilderness for the awe-inspiring Northern Lights viewing experience (seasonal) around a crackling bonfire.

Northern Lights Bonfire
Day 7
Departure — Farewell Breakfast

Enjoy a final farewell breakfast at the resort. Transfer to Vancouver International Airport for your onward flights. Take home a lifetime of memories from Canada's most spectacular wilderness.

Departure Farewell Breakfast
