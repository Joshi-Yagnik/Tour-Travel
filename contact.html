FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 8–12 weeks in advance for international tours, especially during peak season (summer and Christmas). For domestic packages, 4–6 weeks is usually sufficient.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 14 days before departure for most packages. Cancellations 7–14 days before receive a 50% refund. Within 7 days, cancellations are non-refundable. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance.

Do you arrange visa assistance?

Yes! We provide visa guidance and documentation checklists for all destinations. For select countries, we also offer a full visa application assistance service. Contact us for details specific to your destination.
